Here are the most important topics that should be master after ending this course to become a good enough data engineer.
1. Databases and SQL.
2. Database Administration (MySQL, PostgreSQL).
3. Python (Pandas, Numpy).
4. Linux Commands (popular commands, shell scripting).
5. At least the architecture of airflow and kafka and  how they work.
6. Foundation of Big Data.

Some additional skills it's okay to review as well:
1. Docker containers
2. Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures
3. Problem Solving (Leetcode).
